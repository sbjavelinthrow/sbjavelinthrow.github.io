Hi I am danny. I am a comedian and an illustrator.

Pleasance Comedy Reserve 2017.
Finalist of Bath New Act 2017
Finalist of the Leicester Square New Comedian Of The Year 2016
Shortlisted for the BBC Radio New Comedy Award 2014

'Danny Clives turns life's struggles into understated, brilliant observed gags' - Joe Lycett.




In his short career Danny has accomplished quite a lot, acting as tour support for Joe Lycett, appearing in the BBC New Comedy Award, reaching the finals of the Leicester Square New Comedian Of The Year and the Bath Comedy Festival New Act competitions and most recently being handpicked by the Pleasance to peform as part of the prestigious 2017 Comedy Reserve at the Edinburgh Fringe Festival.

He has performed for BBC at the Edinburgh Fringe, the Pleasance Jack Dome, Chortle's Fast Fringe, the 400 seater room of the Leicester Square Theatre, The Glee, The Stand and even done a few gigs in New York USA.

As heard on Radio 2 and Radio 4extra

Firstly, here is some FAQ
Q4; How are your prints made?

A; I use a pencil to draw my idea on a plain piece of white A4 paper, outline it carefully with a fineliner then scan it and add colour in Illustrator. I print the design at home using my Brother printer using the 350gsm paper I buy from a local paper shop. I will then go to the post office, buy a tube and send it off to you. Basically, this operation is all me and no one else.

pencil, fineliner, A3 Ice White 300gsm paper, ink, poster tube, rubber, Adobe Illustrator, my hands This is an A3 print and comes on Ice White 300gsm paper.
