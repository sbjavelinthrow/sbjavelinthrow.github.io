About me

Danny Clives is a comedian, illustrator and boy.
In 2014 he was shortlisted for the BBC New Comedy Award and nominated at the Birmingham Comedy Festival for Best Breakthrough Act.
His jokes have been played on BBC Radio 4 Extra and BBC Radio 2.
In 2015 he did a sell out show for the Birmingham Comedy Festival with his 2 friends and did it again in 2016 for Dave's Leicester Comedy Festival.
He was shortlisted for the Pleasance Comedy Reserve 2016
When he is not writing jokes he spends his time drawing silly illustrations and listening to Simon & Garfunkel.

Firstly, here is some FAQ
Q4; How are your prints made?

A; I use a pencil to draw my idea on a plain piece of white A4 paper, outline it carefully with a fineliner then scan it and add colour in Illustrator. I print the design at home using my Brother printer using the 350gsm paper I buy from a local paper shop. I will then go to the post office, buy a tube and send it off to you. Basically, this operation is all me and no one else.

pencil, fineliner, A3 Ice White 300gsm paper, ink, poster tube, rubber, Adobe Illustrator, my hands This is an A3 print and comes on Ice White 300gsm paper. All of the prints are made at home via my inkjet printer so if you buy one I will print it myself and post it to you myself. All photos by Damien Hyde Photography.
